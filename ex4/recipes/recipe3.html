Pasta Recipe

A detailed description of Pasta Recipe.

Ingredients

  • 400g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the tomato sauce to the pan and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and garnish with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
