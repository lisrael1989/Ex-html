Burger Recipe

A detailed description of Burger Recipe .

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, egg, salt, and pepper.
  2. Form the mixture into 4 patties.
  3. Grill or pan-fry the patties for 5-7 minutes on each side, until cooked through.
  4. Place the patties on the buns and top with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
